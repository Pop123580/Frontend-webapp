# LearnAI - AI Study Timetable User Guide

## Welcome to AI-Powered Study Planning

LearnAI now features an intelligent study timetable generator that creates personalized exam preparation schedules optimized just for you.

## Quick Start

### For Study Plan Users

1. **Go to Study Plan Tab** (paper-turning sound effect)
2. **Add Study Sessions**
   - Subject: e.g., "Mathematics"
   - Topic: e.g., "Calculus Chapter 3-5"
   - Deadline: Select your exam date
   - Priority: Choose high, medium, or low

3. **AI Timetable Appears** (automatically)
   - Once you add one session, the AI card appears below

4. **Click "AI" Button**
   - AI generates your personalized schedule
   - Takes 2-3 seconds to generate

5. **Review Your Schedule**
   - See daily study duration for each subject
   - Check recommended time slots (9 AM, 2 PM, 6 PM)
   - Review focus areas for each session

6. **Download (Optional)**
   - Click "Download CSV" to save your timetable
   - Import to spreadsheet or planner

### For Exam Prep Users

1. **Go to Exam Prep Tab** (paper-turning sound)
2. **Create Exam Plan**
   - Exam name: e.g., "Final Chemistry Exam"
   - Subject: e.g., "Chemistry"
   - Exam date: Select date
   - Syllabus topics: Add what you need to study

3. **Select Your Exam**
   - From the list on the left side

4. **AI Timetable Appears**
   - In the exam details section on the right

5. **Click "AI" Button**
   - Generates exam-specific study schedule
   - Optimized for your exam date

6. **Track Progress**
   - Monitor daily study sessions
   - Update completion status

## Tips for Best Results

### Study Plan Tips

1. **Add Multiple Subjects**
   - More subjects = better optimization
   - AI balances all equally
   - Order doesn't matter

2. **Set Realistic Deadlines**
   - Earlier dates = more daily hours
   - Later dates = spread out study
   - Account for other exams

3. **Use Focus Areas**
   - Be specific: "Chapter 3 questions"
   - Better AI recommendations
   - Easier to track progress

4. **Follow Time Slots**
   - 9 AM: Fresh mind, difficult topics
   - 2 PM: Active time, practice problems
   - 6 PM: Review and consolidation

### Exam Prep Tips

1. **List All Topics**
   - Complete syllabus coverage
   - AI balances everything
   - No topic left behind

2. **Set Exam Date Correctly**
   - Double-check actual exam date
   - AI optimizes for that date
   - Affects all recommendations

3. **Review Regularly**
   - Check timetable daily
   - Mark completed sessions
   - Update as you progress

4. **Download & Print**
   - CSV on wall near desk
   - Physical backup
   - Easy reference

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I edit the generated timetable?**
A: Currently no, but you can download and edit the CSV file.

**Q: How many subjects can I add?**
A: As many as you need. AI optimizes for all of them.

**Q: What if I miss a day?**
A: Just adjust the remaining days. Regenerate for updated schedule.

**Q: Is the timetable saved?**
A: Downloaded CSV is saved. In-app timetables disappear on refresh.

**Q: Can I share my timetable?**
A: Yes! Download CSV and share the file with study group.

**Q: How accurate is the AI recommendation?**
A: Very! Based on study science and spaced repetition principles.

**Q: Can I use different time slots?**
A: Yes! Suggested times are flexible. Adapt to your schedule.

**Q: What if exam date passes?**
A: Error message shown. Update to future date to generate new timetable.

## Troubleshooting

### AI Button Doesn't Work

**Check**
1. Have you added at least 1 subject?
2. Is exam date set and in future?
3. Are you connected to internet?

**Solution**
- Add subjects if missing
- Set future exam date
- Check internet connection
- Refresh page if stuck

### Timetable Looks Wrong

**Check**
1. Is exam date correct?
2. Are all subjects included?
3. Total days reasonable?

**Solution**
- Verify exam date
- Ensure all subjects added
- Generate new timetable

### Audio Not Playing

**Check**
1. Is browser muted?
2. Device volume on?
3. Browser supports audio?

**Solution**
- Unmute browser
- Check device volume
- Try different browser
- Can't disable currently

### Can't Download CSV

**Check**
1. Have you generated a timetable?
2. Is browser blocking downloads?
3. Disk space available?

**Solution**
- Generate timetable first
- Check browser settings
- Allow downloads
- Free up disk space
